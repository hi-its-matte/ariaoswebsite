Aria - The Lightweight and High-Performance Operating System

What is Aria?

Aria is a startup focused on creating and distributing lightweight, high-quality operating systems designed for everyday use. Our goal is to deliver fast, simple, and accessible computing experiences—especially on older or lower-spec hardware—without sacrificing modern usability or essential features.

Which operating systems are available?

We are currently developing several operating systems, each tailored to different types of hardware: Aria Nebula OS Based on Lubuntu Designed for very old PCs, including systems with low RAM and slower processors Lightweight, responsive interface Ideal for basic productivity, web browsing, and educational use Aria Titan OS Based on Ubuntu Optimized for modern computers with more powerful hardware Full-featured desktop experience with a modern UI Pre-installed with essential apps and tools for daily and professional use Strong hardware and software compatibility Aria Titan OS Lite Based on Zorin OS Lite Designed for recent but not high-end PCs Clean, user-friendly interface with low resource consumption Great balance between performance and visual design

Why choose Aria?

Systems tailored for a wide range of hardware Broad compatibility with modern software and devices Pre-installed apps ready for everyday tasks Lightweight and fast, even on aging machines Regular updates with a focus on stability and security A transparent and growing project built for real users
