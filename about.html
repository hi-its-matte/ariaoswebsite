What is Aria?

Aria - The Lightweight and High-Performance Operating System

Aria is a startup focused on creating and distributing lightweight, high-quality operating systems designed for everyday use. Our goal is to deliver fast, simple, and accessible computing experiences—especially on older or lower-spec hardware—without sacrificing modern usability or essential features.

Which operating systems are available?

We are currently developing several operating systems, each tailored to different types of hardware:

  • Aria Nebula OS – Based on Lubuntu. Designed for very old PCs, including low RAM and slower processors. Lightweight, responsive interface ideal for basic productivity, web browsing, and educational use.
  • Aria Titan OS – Based on Debian. Optimized for modern computers with more powerful hardware. Full-featured desktop with modern UI, essential apps pre-installed, strong hardware/software compatibility.
